    About Me

    Hello! I'm Nicholas, a Computer Science student at Monash University based in Melbourne, Australia.

    My passion for software development started in high school after I enrolled in my first programming class and discovered my love for all things tech. After that, I proceeded to enrol in every IT subject that my school had to offer so that I could learn as much as I could about developing software.

    I enjoy working on the occasional side project in my spare time to explore different areas and concepts that I'm interested in. More recently I've been working on a virtual Monash University campus with various building-level text rooms with a couple of fellow Comp Sci/IT students.

    During my new found lockdown time, I've been learning to juggle, playing Stardew Valley, baking, working on the occasional ARG, messing around with a lil bit of crochet, and trying to hone my kalimba skills. I'm taking a heavy project unit this semester so be sure to look out for more stuff coming this year.
